How is this game played?
  • Go on location and tag it with your Street Art Gangs app
  • You can see the tags, avatars of other players and the paroling police man among other things in a 3D version of the game are
  • Collect points for your gang
  • The gang who has the most points when the game ends, wins!
How do I get points?
  • You earn point by your actions. It is also possible to lose points
  • Tagging a location gives 100p
  • Busting a member of another gang 30p
  • Changing status once a day 10p
  • Walking a kilometre within the game area 5p/km
  • Getting busted! -30p
What is the game area?

For now it is nine blocks in the city centre.


How do I start spraying?

Push the icon on "Home" screen. The icon is clear and visible, when you are close enough to tag.

What "Bust" and "Busted" means?

You get busted when a member of a rival gang or a police man interrupts your tagging.

You bust someone by interrupting them by being in close proximity as they try to spray.

What can I do with this app?

Tag locations, follow your own and your gang's progress, set your mood from "Tweaks" and chat with your homies

How do I log out?

You stay logged in unless you log out. You can logout from "Tweaks"
